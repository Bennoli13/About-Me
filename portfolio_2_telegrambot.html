Project Overview

I initiated and led a project with a team of three members to develop the sales asistant bot. Our project leveraged the Telegram module in Python to create a user-friendly and efficient communication channel. Through this system, internal sales team members gained the ability to interact with the AI, streamlining various processes and enhancing productivity.

Objective

The primary objective of this project is to leverage an AI tool, similar to ChatGPT, customized to meet the specific needs of our sales team. This tool aims to streamline customer engagement and provide quick, efficient access to extensive product and service information, thereby reducing the time spent searching through knowledge bases (KBs).

Key outcomes

  • Information Accessibility: Sales team can quickly retrieve relevant information from extensive KBs, enhancing their response time and knowledge.
  • Enhanced Customer Engagement: More informed and timely interactions with customers, leading to improved customer satisfaction and potential sales conversions.
  • Efficiency in Sales Process: Reduction in time spent searching for information, leading to more time for direct sales activities.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Gathering data on common queries and trends, which can inform future product development and sales strategies.

Problem Statement

Our sales team currently faces challenges in efficiently navigating through extensive knowledge bases to find specific product and service information. This leads to prolonged customer interactions and delays in providing accurate responses, affecting sales performance and customer satisfaction.
